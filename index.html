About Us

Trusted Healthcare for Abeokuta

Olufunmi Specialist Hospital is a world-class, reputable health facility located at Asero Estate, Abeokuta. Since October 4, 2010, we have provided high-quality, patient-focused care to individuals and corporate organisations, with services that are accessible and cost-effective for the communities we serve.

With over 15 years of experience, our professionalism, efficiency, tact, and prompt service delivery—combined with a serene and neat environment—have earned us sustained trust from both public and private organisations.

Fully Operational Specialist Hospital

Our hospital has served Abeokuta since 2010, providing specialist departments and reliable diagnostic support around the clock.

Round-the-Clock Patient Care

We deliver 24/7, high-quality healthcare services designed to meet patient needs quickly, respectfully, and professionally.

Trusted by Individuals & Organisations

Our consistent standards, neat environment, and dependable service have earned lasting confidence from families, companies, and institutions.
