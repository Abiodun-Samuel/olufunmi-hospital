What We Are Sourcing

Two Strategic Procurement Streams

Our procurement covers both our operational hospital's ongoing needs and our nursing college expansion.

Nursing College & Training

For our expanding nursing education programme, equipping the next generation of Nigerian healthcare professionals.

  • Nursing simulation manikins (adult, paediatric, infant)
  • Clinical skills training kits & task trainers
  • Anatomical models and educational charts
  • Training room furniture & AV equipment
  • IV therapy, catheterisation, and wound care trainers
  • CPR and basic life support training equipment
  • Nursing textbooks and digital learning resources
  • Stethoscopes, sphygmomanometers (student sets)

Hospital Operations & Expansion

For our operational specialist hospital, upgrading, restocking, and expanding clinical infrastructure.

  • Hospital beds and ward furniture (standard & electric)
  • Operating theatre equipment and instruments
  • Diagnostic imaging equipment (X-ray, ultrasound)
  • Laboratory analysers and reagents
  • Patient monitoring systems (ECG, SpO2, BP)
  • Sterilisation and infection control equipment
  • Medical consumables and disposables (bulk)
  • Pharmacy shelving and dispensing equipment
Collaboration

Partnership Opportunities

We welcome all forms of partnership that support our mission of delivering world-class healthcare and training.

Equipment Supply Agreements

Formal supply contracts for medical and training equipment with agreed quality standards, warranties, and after-sales service.

Training Partnerships

Manufacturer-led training programmes for our clinical staff on equipment use, maintenance, and best practice protocols.

Technical Collaborations

Joint ventures with medical technology companies to pilot new equipment in our hospital environment and contribute to African market data.

Long-term Procurement Contracts

Ongoing supply relationships for consumables, reagents, and disposables with predictable volume commitments and preferred supplier status.

Why Choose Us

Why Partner With Olufunmi Hospital

Established Operational Facility

We are not a new hospital. We are an established specialist facility with over 15 years of operations in Abeokuta, Ogun State. Our existing infrastructure means your equipment will be deployed immediately into a functioning clinical environment.

Long-term Buyer Relationship

Our expansion into nursing education creates sustained, recurring procurement needs, not a one-off purchase. We are seeking long-term supplier relationships with companies who understand the African healthcare market.

Nigeria's Strategic Healthcare Market

Nigeria is Sub-Saharan Africa's largest economy with a rapidly expanding middle class and growing demand for quality healthcare. Abeokuta is a major Ogun State hub with excellent connectivity to Lagos and across the region.

International Standards Focus

We prioritise ISO-certified, CE-marked, and FDA-cleared equipment. As we expand our training programme, international accreditation is a key goal, making quality supplier partnerships essential.

Ongoing & Growing Procurement Needs

From consumables and reagents to major equipment, our procurement calendar is active throughout the year. The Nursing College adds an entirely new category of training equipment with recurring supply requirements each academic year.
