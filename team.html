Hospital Leadership

Meet the founders leading our clinical excellence and long-term healthcare vision.

Dr. Erinoso Adebayo

Obstetrician & Gynaecologist | Co-Founder & Medical Director
20+ Years Experience
MBChB OOU
WACS Member

Dr. Erinoso Adebayo is an experienced Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with over 20 years in women’s health, pregnancy care, and reproductive medicine. He earned his MBChB from Olabisi Onabanjo University, completed residency training at Federal Medical Centre Abeokuta, and is a Member of the West African College of Surgeons (WACS).

Dr. Erinoso-Adekunle Ayotunde

Family Physician | Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer
15+ Years Practice
FWACP NPMCN Fellow
MPH / MBA Public Health & Business

Dr. Erinoso-Adekunle Ayotunde is an experienced Family Physician with over 15 years of clinical practice, dedicated to delivering comprehensive, patient-centered care across all stages of life. Her expertise includes preventive healthcare, early diagnosis, chronic disease management, and coordinated specialist care.

She is passionate about empowering patients through personalized treatment plans, health education, and compassionate care.

Dr. Erinoso-Adekunle holds an MBChB from Olabisi Onabanjo University and completed her residency training at Federal Medical Centre, Abeokuta. She is a Fellow of the National Postgraduate Medical College of Nigeria (NPMCN), graduating as the overall best candidate in 2016. She also holds a Master of Public Health (MPH) from Obafemi Awolowo University and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Quantic School of Business and Technology.
