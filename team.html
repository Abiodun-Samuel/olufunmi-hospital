Dr. Erinoso Adebayo

Obstetrician & Gynaecologist | Co-Founder & Medical Director
20+ Years Experience
MBChB OOU
WACS Member

Dr. Erinoso Adebayo is an experienced Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with over 20 years in women’s health, pregnancy care, and reproductive medicine. He earned his MBChB from Olabisi Onabanjo University, completed residency training at Federal Medical Centre Abeokuta, and is a Member of the West African College of Surgeons (WACS).

Dr. Erinoso-Adekunle Ayotunde

Family Physician | Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer
15+ Years Practice
FWACP NPMCN Fellow
MPH / MBA Public Health & Business

Dr. Erinoso-Adekunle Ayotunde is an experienced Family Physician with over 15 years of clinical practice, dedicated to comprehensive, patient-centered care across all stages of life. She holds an MBChB from Olabisi Onabanjo University, is a Fellow of NPMCN (overall best candidate, 2016), and also holds MPH and MBA degrees.
