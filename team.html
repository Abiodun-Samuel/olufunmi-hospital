Dr. Olufunmi Adeleke

Founder & Medical Director
20+ Years Practice
2008 Year Founded
10k+ Patients Served

Dr. Olufunmi Adeleke founded Olufunmi Specialist Hospital Ltd. in 2008 with a vision to bring world-class specialist healthcare to Abeokuta. With over 20 years of clinical practice in internal medicine and hospital management, she has guided the hospital to become one of the most trusted specialist centres in Ogun State. She now leads the hospital's strategic expansion into nursing education and clinical training.

Mr. Adewale Ogundimu oversees all administrative, financial, and operational functions of Olufunmi Specialist Hospital.
