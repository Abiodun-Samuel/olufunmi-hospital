Medical Services

Our Medical Services

We offer a comprehensive range of specialist healthcare services, delivered with clinical excellence, compassion, and world-class standards.

Clinical Services

Comprehensive Specialist Medical Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive adult medicine covering the full spectrum of internal conditions. Our physicians provide expert diagnosis, ongoing management, and specialist referrals.

  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Infectious disease treatment
  • Preventive care and health screening
  • Specialist referral coordination
Book Appointment

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Full antenatal, intrapartum, and postnatal care in a safe, supportive environment. Our maternity team is experienced, compassionate, and available 24/7.

  • Antenatal booking and monitoring
  • Normal and high-risk deliveries
  • Postnatal ward care
  • Family planning services
Book Appointment

Paediatrics

Dedicated paediatric services from newborns through adolescence. Our child health specialists provide expert diagnosis and treatment in a friendly, child-centred environment.

  • Newborn and neonatal assessment
  • Childhood illness diagnosis and treatment
  • Growth and developmental monitoring
  • Immunisation and vaccination services
Book Appointment

Surgery

A range of general and specialist surgical procedures performed in our modern, fully equipped operating theatre. Our surgical team upholds the highest standards of patient safety.

  • General surgical procedures
  • Hernia and abdominal surgery
  • Gynaecological surgical procedures
  • Emergency surgical intervention
Book Appointment

Diagnostics & Laboratory

In-house laboratory and diagnostic services providing fast, accurate results. Our clinical team uses diagnostic findings to guide evidence-based treatment decisions.

  • Full blood count and biochemistry panels
  • Microbiology and culture services
  • Urinalysis and hormonal testing
  • Ultrasound imaging services

Radiology, Pharmacy & Support

We provide essential support services including pharmaceutical supplies, laboratory services, and radiological services such as ECG and ultrasound scans for coordinated patient care.

  • Laboratory investigations with reliable turnaround
  • Radiological services including ECG
