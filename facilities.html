Our Facilities

Clinical Spaces

World-Class Clinical Facilities

All our operational departments are fully equipped and staffed. Hover over each facility to learn more.

Consultation Rooms

Private, well-equipped consultation rooms for specialist appointments. Designed for patient comfort and clinical efficiency.

Operating Theatre

Fully equipped operating theatre with modern anaesthesia systems, surgical instruments, and a trained theatre team for a range of procedures.

Maternity Ward

Dedicated maternity ward with antenatal and postnatal care bays, delivery rooms, and neonatal monitoring. Staffed 24/7 by experienced midwives and obstetricians.

Diagnostic Laboratory

In-house clinical laboratory providing haematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and urinalysis services. Fast, accurate results for timely clinical decisions.

Pharmacy

Well-stocked in-patient and outpatient pharmacy dispensing a wide range of medications. Staffed by qualified pharmacists with access to a broad formulary.

Infrastructure Expansion

Expanding Our Training Infrastructure

Our established hospital provides the perfect clinical platform for nursing education. The Nursing College expansion will add purpose-built training spaces directly integrated with our operational wards and departments, giving students real clinical exposure from day one.

The expansion includes a dedicated simulation laboratory equipped with high-fidelity nursing manikins, clinical skills training stations, lecture rooms, and a resource library. We are currently procuring world-class training equipment from international manufacturers, including at Canton Fair Phase 3, May 2026.
