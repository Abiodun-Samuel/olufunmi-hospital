About Olufunmi
Specialist Hospital

Established in Abeokuta since 2008. Over 15 years of world-class specialist healthcare, now expanding our capacity with nursing education.

Our History

More Than a Hospital: A Healthcare Legacy

Olufunmi Specialist Hospital Ltd. was founded in 2008 with a clear vision: to bring world-class specialist medical care to Abeokuta and the people of Ogun State. Located in the heart of Asero Estate, the hospital grew steadily from its founding years into a fully operational multi-department specialist facility serving thousands of patients annually.

Over the past 15 years we have expanded our specialist departments, upgraded our diagnostic capabilities, and built a team of over 20 dedicated doctors, nurses, and support staff. We have served more than 10,000 patients and established Olufunmi as one of the most trusted healthcare providers in Ogun State.

In 2024, we announced the next phase of our development: the expansion of our hospital into a full Nursing College and clinical training centre, extending our impact beyond patient care into the development of Nigeria's future healthcare professionals.

Our Journey

15 Years of Progress

2008

Hospital Founded

Olufunmi Specialist Hospital Ltd. opens its doors in Asero Estate, Abeokuta, beginning with general medicine, maternity, and outpatient departments. A vision to deliver world-class healthcare to Ogun State takes its first steps.

2012

Expansion into Specialist Departments

The hospital expands its clinical scope with dedicated specialist departments: surgery, paediatrics, and diagnostic laboratory services. Investment in modern equipment significantly upgrades diagnostic capacity.

2016

Facility Upgrade & Accreditation

Major facility renovation and equipment upgrade programme completed. The hospital achieves key accreditation milestones, reinforcing its position as a trusted specialist centre in Ogun State.

2020

10,000 Patients Served

Olufunmi Specialist Hospital celebrates the milestone of serving 10,000 patients, a testament to the trust the Abeokuta community has placed in our care. The hospital continues to grow through word-of-mouth recommendation.

2024

Nursing College Expansion Announced

Our established hospital announces its strategic expansion into nursing education and clinical training. A Nursing College that will leverage our existing clinical infrastructure to develop the next generation of Nigerian healthcare professionals. International procurement partnerships begin.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver world-class specialist healthcare to the people of Abeokuta and Ogun State, and to expand access to quality clinical training for the next generation of Nigerian healthcare professionals, through our established hospital and growing Nursing College.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted specialist hospital and clinical training institution in Ogun State, known for international quality standards, patient dignity, and the professional excellence of our medical graduates.

Our Values

Excellence in clinical practice. Compassion in patient care. Integrity in all decisions. Innovation in medical education. Partnership with international institutions. Commitment to the communities we serve.

Credibility & Standards

Accreditation & Affiliations

Olufunmi Specialist Hospital Ltd. is a registered specialist hospital in Ogun State, operating under the regulatory framework of the Nigerian Federal Ministry of Health and the Ogun State Ministry of Health. Our clinical practices conform to national and international standards for patient safety, infection control, and quality of care.
