About the Fair

The World's Largest Trade Fair

The China Import and Export Fair, known as the Canton Fair, is held twice annually in Guangzhou, China. It is the world's largest international trade event, attracting over 200,000 buyers from more than 200 countries and regions.

Phase 3 (May 1-5) covers medical devices, healthcare equipment, sporting goods, and educational materials, making it the ideal venue for hospitals and healthcare institutions to source quality suppliers at competitive prices.

For African healthcare buyers, Canton Fair offers unparalleled access to ISO-certified and CE-marked medical products direct from manufacturers, without the margin of intermediary distributors.

"The Canton Fair is where established African healthcare institutions come to build long-term relationships with the world's most competitive medical equipment manufacturers, and Olufunmi Specialist Hospital will be there."
